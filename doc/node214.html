General approach for solving the CFD equations

The fields to be determined (velocity, pressure, temperature, turbulent kinetic energy....) will be obtained by solving the conservation laws (conservation of mass, of momentum...) in their transient form. Steady state solutions are obtained by continuing a transient calculation up to a point at which the solution does not significantly change any more.
